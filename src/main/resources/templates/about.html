About Test Data Team

We as Test Data Management team we strive to support almost every project across Absa with required transactional test data to enable effective testing.

The online Dashboard is designed to provide instant data. Functionalities are gradually being developed and added on the dashboard through different sprints

Our service catalogue includes:

Data Creation:

  • Cheque Accounts
  • Savings Account
  • Credit Cards
  • Personal Loans
  • Adding Pre-approved
  • Linking virtual combi cards

Outside Everyday Banking

We are unable to create data for applications like: Home Loan, AVAF however we can source whatever is available on the mainframe

We have limited access and knowledge to different systems, however we committed to assist were possible.

For any further requirements or feedback you can also email us on Test.Data.Management@absa.africa
